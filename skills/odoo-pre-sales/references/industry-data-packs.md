# Industry Data Packs

Use the company research and user answers to choose an industry data pack. Keep only relevant pack details in the generated demo.

## Retail

- Product variants, categories, barcode/SKU, POS config, website shop, stock by store/warehouse, promotions if relevant, returns/exchanges, sales by channel.

## Manufacturing

- Products, BOMs, work centers, manufacturing orders, components, stock moves, quality checks, production cost/reporting.

## Services

- Leads/opportunities, quotations, projects/tasks, timesheets, service products, invoices, customer portal, SLA/helpdesk if needed.

## Distribution

- Vendors, purchase orders, warehouses, routes, replenishment rules, sales orders, deliveries, carrier mock, inventory aging.

## Food and Beverage

- POS tables/sessions, menus/products, variants/options, taxes, kitchen/preparation status if available, inventory consumption, shift closing.

## Education

- Leads/admissions, contacts/students/guardians, courses/products, invoices/payments, schedules/events, portal communication.

## Rules

- Pick one primary pack and optional secondary pack.
- Document chosen pack in `solution-mapping.md` and `data-manifest.md`.
- Do not overbuild unrelated industry features. The demo should stay coherent and presentable.
